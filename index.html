Welcome to the Bish-Bosh game!

This is a fun, interactive game where you can customize the rules yourself. Simply enter two numbers — one for "Bish" and another for "Bosh." The game will then count through a sequence of numbers, replacing any number divisible by your "Bish" number with the word "Bish," and any number divisible by your "Bosh" number with the word "Bosh." If a number is divisible by both, you'll see "Bish-Bosh!" Try it out by entering your own numbers below and watch the results update automatically!
